Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PromptForge ("we," "us," "our") operates aipromptsforge.site (the "Service"). This policy explains what information we collect when you use PromptForge, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect 2. How we use your information 3. Third-party services we use 4. Cookies and advertising 5. Data retention 6. Your rights and choices 7. Children's privacy 8. Security 9. Changes to this policy 10. Contact us

1. Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account information: when you sign up, we collect your email address and, if you sign in with Google or Discord, the basic profile information those providers share with us (typically your name, email, and profile picture).
  • Usage data: we track how many prompts you generate each month (to enforce free-plan limits), which prompt templates and categories you use, and which prompts or collections you save.
  • Content you provide: the text you enter into our prompt generators and Prompt Studio fields is processed to generate results, and may be temporarily logged for abuse prevention and quality purposes.
  • Technical data: we automatically collect your IP address, browser type, and device information, primarily for rate-limiting and fraud prevention.
  • Payment information: if you subscribe to Premium, payment is processed entirely by Paystack. We do not collect or store your card details — Paystack handles that directly and shares with us only what's needed to confirm your subscription status (e.g. that payment succeeded, your plan tier).

2. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including generating prompts and enforcing usage limits.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Process Premium subscription payments and manage billing.
  • Communicate with you about your account, support requests, or important changes to the Service.
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Improve the Service based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-party services we use

PromptForge is built on top of the following third-party services, each of which processes certain data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — our authentication and database provider. Your account details and saved content are stored on Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Cloudflare — hosts our website and backend (Workers), and provides security/performance services (DDoS protection, CDN).
  • Google (Gemini API) — the text you submit to our prompt generators is sent to Google's Gemini API to produce results. Google's own privacy policy governs how they process that data on their end.
  • Paystack — processes Premium subscription payments. We never see or store your full card details.
  • Adsterra — our advertising partner, which may use cookies to serve ads on free-tier pages. See the Cookies section below.

4. Cookies and advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in and remember basic preferences. Our advertising partner, Adsterra, may also use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve and measure ads on pages where they appear. Premium subscribers do not see ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect how the Service functions.

5. Data retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we're required to retain certain records (e.g. payment records) for legal or accounting purposes.

6. Your rights and choices

You can access, update, or delete your account information at any time from your Dashboard, or by contacting us directly. Depending on your location, you may also have rights to request a copy of your data, object to certain processing, or request that we restrict how we use it. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

7. Children's privacy

PromptForge is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have, we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication practices, and access controls on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you directly.

10. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, reach out through our Support page or email us at support@aipromptsforge.site.

Quick summary, in plain language:

We collect your email, what you type into our generators, and basic usage stats — just enough to run the product, stop abuse, and process payments. We don't sell your data. Google's Gemini API processes your prompt text to generate results. Paystack handles payments; we never see your card number.
