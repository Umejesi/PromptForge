Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PromptForge ("we," "us," "our") operates aipromptsforge.site (the "Service"). This policy explains what information we collect when you use PromptForge, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect 2. How we use your information 3. Third-party services we use 4. Cookies and advertising 5. Data retention 6. Your rights and choices 7. Children's privacy 8. Security 9. Changes to this policy 10. Contact us

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address, and if you sign in with Google or Discord, the basic profile info those providers share (name, email, profile picture).
  • Usage data: how many prompts you generate each month, which templates and categories you use, and which prompts or collections you save.
  • Content you provide: text you enter into our generators and Prompt Studio fields is processed to generate results.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information, primarily for rate-limiting and fraud prevention.
  • Payment information: Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Paystack. We never see or store your card details.

2. How we use your information

To provide and operate the Service, authenticate your account, process Premium billing, communicate about your account or support requests, detect abuse, and improve the Service using aggregated, anonymized usage patterns. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-party services we use

  • Supabase — authentication and database.
  • Cloudflare — hosting, security, and performance.
  • Google (Gemini API) — processes the text you submit to generate prompt results.
  • Paystack — processes Premium subscription payments.
  • Adsterra — our advertising partner, which may use cookies to serve ads on free-tier pages.

4. Cookies and advertising

We use cookies to keep you signed in and remember preferences. Adsterra may use cookies to serve and measure ads. Premium subscribers see no ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data retention

We retain your account information while your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within a reasonable period, except where required to retain records for legal or accounting purposes.

6. Your rights and choices

You can access, update, or delete your account information from your Dashboard, or by contacting us. Depending on your location, you may have rights to request a copy of your data or object to certain processing.

7. Children's privacy

PromptForge is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication practices, and access controls on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will update the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact us

Questions? Reach out through our Support page or email supportaipromptsforge.site@gmail.com.
