Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains how refunds work for PromptForge Premium subscriptions, billed monthly through Paystack.

Cancel anytime

Cancel your Premium subscription anytime from your Dashboard, or by contacting Support. Cancelling stops all future billing immediately. You keep Premium access until the end of the period you already paid for.

Our standard policy

Because Premium unlocks unlimited usage immediately upon payment, subscription fees are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started — standard for digital subscription services where access is granted instantly.

When we do offer a refund

  • You were charged in error (duplicate charge, or billed after you'd already cancelled).
  • A technical fault on our end prevented you from using Premium for a significant portion of your billing period, and we couldn't resolve it.
  • You were charged despite never successfully activating Premium.

If any of these apply, contact us within 7 days of the charge and we'll review it promptly.

How to request a refund

Email supportaipromptsforge.site@gmail.com or use our Support form with your account email and the reason for your request. We aim to respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds go back to your original payment method via Paystack, typically within 5–10 business days.

Free plan

Since Free (10 prompts/month) requires no payment, there's nothing to refund — simply don't upgrade, or downgrade at any time.

Quick summary: Cancel anytime, no future charges. Refunds are given for billing errors or real technical failures — not simply for "changed my mind." Reach out within 7 days if something went wrong.
