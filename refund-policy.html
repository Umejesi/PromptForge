Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains how refunds work for PromptForge Premium subscriptions, billed monthly through Paystack.

Cancel anytime

You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time from your Dashboard, or by contacting Support. Cancelling stops all future billing immediately. You'll keep Premium access until the end of the billing period you already paid for.

Our standard policy

Because Premium unlocks unlimited usage immediately upon payment, subscription fees are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started. This is standard practice for digital subscription services where access is granted instantly.

When we do offer a refund

We'll happily refund you in the following cases:

  • You were charged in error (e.g. duplicate charge, billed after you'd already cancelled).
  • A technical fault on our end prevented you from using Premium features for a significant portion of your billing period, and we were unable to resolve it.
  • You were charged despite never successfully activating your Premium account.

If any of these apply to you, contact us within 7 days of the charge and we'll review it promptly.

How to request a refund

Email support@aipromptsforge.site or use our Support form with your account email and the reason for your request. We aim to respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method via Paystack, typically within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

Free trial

Since our Free plan (10 prompts/month) requires no payment, there's no charge to refund if you decide PromptForge isn't for you — simply don't upgrade, or downgrade at any time.

Quick summary:

Cancel anytime, no future charges. Refunds are given for billing errors or real technical failures on our side — not simply for "changed my mind" after using the month. Reach out within 7 days if something went wrong.
